Challenge #0

This is a simple, beginner level challenge with guided instructions for you to follow along. We hope you enjoy this little primer!

Opening Chrome DevTools

Once the webpage has loaded, you can press "Ctrl + Shift + I" on the laptop you are using, which should open up the developer menu.

Navigating to the JS Console

Once you have opened Chrome DevTools, you can click on the "Console" tab, where there will be a text-based interface for the website. 

Getting the flag

As this is the first challenge, you will receive the flag directly in the console. Do you see it too? If so, write it down on your form.
