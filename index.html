Welcome!

This is the homepage of 3.10 Nehemiah's escape room. It is in the format of a "CTF" challenge. CTF means Capture the Flag, where the prime objective is to obtain flags based on clues. 
CTFs usually involve advanced cybersecurity skills. However, this CTF has been specifically designed for the beginner. 
Good luck to all!
